About Us

We are a Danish organization promoting design and creative industries—now expanding into the digital app market to solve daily problems with practical mobile solutions.

Design Society (fond) originated as a non-profit organization dedicated to strengthening design culture and the creative industries. Today, we’re building on that foundation by launching a Digital Division—bringing the same commitment to quality, craft, and purpose into software.

Our mission remains consistent: empower people through design. We now apply that mission to mobile products that help users work smarter, stay organized, and simplify everyday routines.

How we work

  • Corporate-grade reliability: stable, secure, and maintainable
  • Focused on utility, clarity, and real-world outcomes
